Our Products

Victory Supplements™ proudly sells Magnum Nutraceuticals, formulated using 100% Pharmaceutical Grade raw ingredients to ensure the best results.

Feed Grade

This grade of raw material meets the standards set for animal consumption. The impurities that animals can consume with no negative side effects are not the same as what humans can tolerate. There is no law stopping supplement brands from using feed grade raw materials!

Food Grade

This grade of raw material meets the standards set for human consumption. Ingredients can fall within 80% of the label claim and still meet these requirements. Most other suppliments use food grade raw materials!

Pharmaceutical Grade

This is the raw material grade that is used in hospitals and university studies. These products are required to be in excess of 99% purity, with no contaminants, micro-bacterial content, binders, fillers, excipients, or any other unknown substances.
